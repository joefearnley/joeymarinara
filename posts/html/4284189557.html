Jaspare's Pizza

It’s Friday night, band practice on the schedule, what better way to feed yourself and friends by ordering up some pies? This particular night we decided to go with Jaspare’s. This place, similar to Chicago Style, had quite the reputation around these parts (and god damn it if that didn’t hold completely strong in my eyes). There are a few locations around the area although after driving by the Portage location the other day on my way to hockey it didn’t look like that particular spot had seen any action in a while. 

This pizza had it all. Solid on the crust, solid on the cheese and that sauce made me want to lick every dropping on the box. I wasn’t there to order so I don’t know what the options were but we ended up with a medium-sized crust (typical size I just don’t know how else to put it). It lived up to the hype across the board. After stuffing our faces and finishing up band practice, we headed out for a few drinks and to tell more people about the bomb-ass pizza I had earlier. Unfortunately I didn’t get any pictures of the pizza this time around. Next time gadget, next time.

Finally I round out the night over at my next-door neighbors house who is having a party. One of the last dudes there was a guys wearing a Jaspare’s Pizza sweatshirt. I think it still had some flour on it from that nights shift. I told him how awesome the pizza was and that I was starting this blog to rate pizza and…well I was drunk and probably sounded like an idiot. He kindly smiled like he cared and agreed that the pizza was good. I’ll take what I can get.

Final rating: 5 slices
