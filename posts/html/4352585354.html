Brewster's

Fall is here and football season is upon us. Rachel got a craving for some BBQ chicken tenders from Brewster’s so Dave and I decided to come along. Frankly I didn’t even know this place even had pizza on the menu but they did and Dave talked me into it.

I honestly don’t remember if there were options as far as the toppings were concerned but I ended up with pepperoni. The pizza itself was small, I’d say 6 or 7 inches and was thin crust. Probably the only pizza in town in which I could eat the entire thing, I have to say it wasn’t half bad. I’m sure they got most of the ingredients from Gordon’s or something but the combination of them, again, wasn’t half bad.

I can’t say I would suggest Brewster’s pizza to anyone until they change how they serve it. Being that there are a few of these around the area this may be the case one day. But for me on this Saturday afternoon when I couldn’t decide on anything else on the menu it suited my needs just fine.

Final rating: 2 slices
